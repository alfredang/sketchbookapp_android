# Changelog

All notable changes to Sketchbook Studio for Android.

## [1.0] — 2026-07-06 · versionCode 1

### Added
- First Android release, mirroring the iOS Sketchbook Studio iPad app.
- 20-brush library (Inking / Sketching / Painting) with pencil grades 2H–6B and a Stardust particle brush.
- Layers with opacity, lock, hide, rename, reorder and thumbnails.
- Multi-page sketchbooks with pages sidebar and page navigation.
- Vector shapes (10 kinds) with move / resize / rotate / flip and colour fill.
- Flood fill of enclosed areas.
- Symmetry drawing (vertical, horizontal, quad) with guides.
- Adjustments (hue/saturation/brightness, colour balance, blur, noise, sharpen, mosaic) and one-tap filters.
- 8 page templates (ruled, grid, dot grid, isometric, storyboard, music staff, ring file) and 4 paper colours.
- Pinch zoom and pan, two-finger tap undo, three-finger tap redo.
- Autosaving gallery with favorites, duplicate and delete.
